Never miss a LETUS assignment deadline.

Automatically scans all your enrolled courses on LETUS — the LMS used at Tokyo University of Science — and keeps you on top of every due date with browser notifications and a clean popup view.

■ Key Features

[Auto Scan]
Stay up to date without lifting a finger. While you're logged into LETUS, the extension periodically fetches assignments from all your enrolled courses automatically.

[Deadline List by Period]
Click the toolbar icon to see all upcoming deadlines grouped by "This Week," "Next Week," and "Later." A summary badge shows counts at a glance.

[Browser Notifications]
Get reminded before a deadline hits — even when you're working in another tab.

[Open Assignments Directly]
Click any assignment card to open the corresponding LETUS page in a new tab instantly.

[Hide & Restore]
Clean up your list by hiding assignments you've already submitted or don't need to track. Restore them anytime.

[Dashboard View]
Need the full picture? Open the dashboard for a wide-screen view of all assignments across every course.

■ How to Use

1. Install the extension
2. Log in to LETUS (letus.ed.tus.ac.jp)
3. Click the toolbar icon to open the popup
4. Press "Scan" to fetch your assignments
5. After that, scans run automatically on a schedule

■ Target Users

Students enrolled at Tokyo University of Science who use LETUS.

■ Privacy

· Assignment data is stored locally on your device only
· No assignment data is sent to external servers
· The extension only activates on letus.ed.tus.ac.jp

■ Disclaimer

This extension is an independent student-built tool and is not affiliated with Tokyo University of Science. No official support is provided.
